# Research Strategy

Working plan for building the first evidence-based version of the family tree.

## Executive Assessment

### What Is Built
- A fresh genealogy vault created from the repository template on 2026-03-22.
- Core files and working folders are in place.
- No person files or sourced family entries have been added yet.

### What Is Credible
- No factual genealogical claims have been established yet.
- Any future family-memory entries should be marked `(unverified)` until supported by sources.

### Where the Biggest Leverage Lies
1. Collect names of parents and grandparents.
2. Collect dates and places from memory and home documents.
3. Identify the earliest known ancestor with a specific location.

## Research Plan

### Phase 1: Baseline Intake
**Objective**: Build the first working tree from known family information.
**Tasks**: Add names, approximate dates, locations, and relationships to [[Family_Tree]]; list existing documents in [[Data_Inventory]].
**Deliverables**: First-pass tree and home-source inventory.
**Done when**: Parents and grandparents are listed and at least one family line has a place attached.

### Phase 2: Open Web Research
**Objective**: Use free public sources to extend the strongest documented line.
**Tasks**: Search for graves, obituaries, civil registrations, church records, memorial databases, and local archive indexes.
**Dependencies**: Requires at least one target person with a name plus approximate date or place.
**Done when**: A research pass adds confirmed evidence or logs strong negative results.

### Phase 3: External Data and Human Action
**Objective**: Gather records not available through open web search.
**Tasks**: Request scans from relatives, revisit family photo boxes, contact archives if needed.
**Dependencies**: Open questions should already be narrowed.

## Citation Standard

- Record enough detail to find the source again.
- Prefer direct links to the source page or archive catalog entry.
- Distinguish between family knowledge, secondary summaries, and primary records.
