# Open Questions

Research gaps and intake questions, organized by priority.

## High Priority

### 1. What home documents already exist?
- **The question**: We do not yet know which certificates, letters, military papers, photos, or notebooks are available.
- **Minimum records needed**: A simple inventory of what exists at home.
- **Next step**: List them in [[Data_Inventory]].

### 2. What are the names and details of the remaining great-grandparents?
- **The question**: Several parents of current grandparents remain unidentified.
- **Minimum records needed**: Family memory, grave markers, certificates, funeral cards, or village records.
- **Next step**: Ask specifically about the parents of Василь Ткач, Катерина Колбун, Надія Сорока, and the mother of Володимир Деркач.

### 3. What are the marriage details for the grandparent couples?
- **The question**: Marriage date and place would help identify records and jurisdictions.
- **Minimum records needed**: Marriage certificate, church memory, anniversary photos, or family oral history.
- **Next step**: Record approximate marriage year and village for each couple.

## Medium Priority

### 4. Which family line should be researched first?
- **The question**: Once baseline names are entered, we should choose the line with the best dates and locations.
- **Search strategy**: Prioritize the earliest known ancestor with a specific place and approximate birth or death period.
- **Solvability**: HIGH
- **Payoff**: HIGH
